Everything you need to know about OpenSourceGen

OpenSourceGen is a free AI image and video generator powered by open-source models like Z-Image-Turbo, HunyuanImage 3.0, and LTX-2.3. Generate AI images from text, edit existing images, fuse multiple images together, and create AI videos—all with full control over parameters like resolution, seed, samplers, and frame rate. No subscription required.
No account is needed to start generating. You can create AI images and videos immediately with daily free credits. Sign up optionally to unlock higher daily credit limits, save your generations to a personal library, purchase credits, and share your creations via public links.
Every user receives free daily credits that reset each day. Free users get up to 1,000 daily credits—enough to generate dozens of AI images. If you need more, you can purchase credit packs starting at $5 with no subscription commitment, or subscribe for up to 4,000 daily credits plus priority queue access.
OpenSourceGen offers three models: Z-Image-Turbo for fast, low-cost image generation with 35+ samplers and style LoRAs (Realistic Snapshot, Pixel Art, 35mm Photo, and more); HunyuanImage 3.0 for high-quality images with 33 resolution presets, image editing, and multi-image fusion; and LTX-2.3 for AI video generation with customizable resolution up to 2K, frame rate, duration, and optional audio.
Yes. The LTX-2.3 model supports both text-to-video and image-to-video generation. You can create videos in SD, HD, Full HD, or 2K resolution at up to 60 fps with configurable frame count and duration. Videos can optionally include generated audio. Pro subscribers can generate videos up to 20 seconds long at 2K resolution.
You get full control over the generation process. For images: resolution, seed, sampling steps, sampler type, scheduler, and style LoRAs with adjustable strength. For videos: resolution, frame count, FPS, negative prompts, and audio toggle. HunyuanImage 3.0 supports 33 native aspect ratios from ultra-wide to tall portrait formats. You can also generate up to 3 images per prompt in a single batch.
Credit packs start at $5 for 2,100 credits (5% bonus) and go up to $50 for 26,000 credits (30% bonus). Subscribers earn extra bonus credits on every purchase—Basic subscribers get +20% and Pro subscribers get +30%. Optional subscriptions are available at $25/month (Basic) and $50/month (Pro) for higher daily credit limits and priority generation queue.
Yes—you can generate AI images and videos every day at no cost using your free daily credits. There is no mandatory subscription and no hidden fees. Free credits reset daily so you can keep creating. Pay-as-you-go credit packs are available if you want to generate more, but they are entirely optional.
Yes. HunyuanImage 3.0 supports AI image editing where you upload a source image and describe the changes you want. It also supports multi-image fusion, which lets you combine exactly two images into a new AI-generated composition. Both modes give you full control over seed and resolution.
Every user gets a unique referral code. When someone signs up with your code and completes their first generation, you earn 500 bonus credits and they receive 250 bonus credits. You can earn referral rewards up to 3 times per day, with a maximum of 50 total referrals.
All generations are private by default and only visible to you. You can choose to share individual images or videos via public share links. Content moderation is applied automatically to protect against harmful outputs, and sharing is blocked for flagged content.
